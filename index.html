Great People make Great Organizations

FTR excels by its pragmatic, solution focused approach, clear and no-nonsense communication and our engaging, empathic and respectful approach.

What we do

When change is mandatory and interventions of Leadership and interim management are not successful it’s time for a totally different approach. Traditional, Old School approach by finding compromises and consensice are at best, bringing only a part of a (short term) solution and is not sustainable in today’s agile markets.

FTR brings proven - out of the box - sustainable value adding solutions,

  • flexibility that enables companies to up- and downscale at anytime without costly restructuring costs
  • performance driven management that is capable to drive and develop the business
  • continious improvement to stay connected with clients, market and company objectives
  • improve employer branding, to attract, develop and retain talents
  • performance management with clear KPI’s translating to remuneration packages rewarding value creation focusing on individual and team performance linked to company results

Our Approach

In most, if not all, cases we have been involved in, we noticed a lack of

  1. Competent leadership
  2. Setting the wrong priorities
  3. Non-engaging communication

The above are FTR strongholds, clients find our approach very refreshing, transparent and motivating to work with. FTR does not offer a ‘one size fits all’ approach, but offers pragmatic, tailor made, value adding solutions respecting company legacy and with empathy for the individual employee. After defining and agreeing to the change project plan, setting priorities and objectives a project team of hand picked SME’s will be appointed.

FTR project team members are pleasant people to work with, empathic, result driven senior executives within their domains and with a proven change management record. These domains include Manufacturing, Operations, Supply Chain, Maintenance, Finance, IT, HR, General Management, Procurement, Facility Management and more.
